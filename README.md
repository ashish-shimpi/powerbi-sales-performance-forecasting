# Power Bi Sales Performance and Forecasting Dashboard
An end-to-end Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard featuring time series forecasting, advanced DAX modelling, and actionable business insights using the Superstore dataset.

## Project Overview

- This project delivers an end-to-end Sales Performance and Forecasting solution built in Power BI. 
- The solution integrates data modelling (star schema), advanced DAX measures, and Power BI forecasting analytics to transform raw retail data into strategic, decision-ready insights.

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Time Series Analysis
- Data Modelling (Star Schema)
- CSV Data Source

## Key Achievements

- Designed a star schema data model for efficient performance optimisation
- Built custom DAX measures for KPI tracking and year-over-year comparison
- Developed a 15-day sales forecast to support short-term operational planning
- Delivered an executive-ready interactive dashboard for business stakeholders

## Business Objective

- To help a retail business:
- Monitor overall sales and profitability performance
- Identify top-performing regions, segments, and categories
- Analyse monthly trends and seasonal behaviour
- Forecast short-term sales to support inventory and operational planning
- Enable data-driven regional and strategic decision-making

## Key Business Insights
## Sales & Profit Performance

- Total Sales: $1.6M
- Total Profit: $175K
- Clear upward trend observed in late 2020
- Profit volatility highlights margin optimisation opportunities

## Regional Performance

- West region contributes the highest share (~33%)
- South region underperforms relative to others
- Enables targeted regional sales strategies

## Product & Category Analysis

- Technology category drives strong revenue
- Phones and Chairs are top-performing subcategories
- Opportunity to optimise underperforming product lines

## Payment Behaviour

- COD dominates (43%)
- Digital payment adoption at 35%
- Insight useful for financial planning and risk management

## Time Series Forecasting

- Built a 15-day sales forecast using historical trend modelling
- Identified seasonality and demand spikes
- Supports short-term planning and inventory optimisation
